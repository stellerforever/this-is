wish

i wish i could be held by everyone.

when i pass by, i'm never sure if i should stop and stare, drink it all in so i never forget or pretend you're a stranger.

i wish i could wrap myself in happy feelings forever.

i wish the walls weren't too high to scale.

i wish in these quiet moments, i could feel safety in knowing the world will come back and scoop me in her arms again.

i wish i wasn't afraid of being alone forever.

i wish i didn't get lost in happiness.

i wish i could still find myself in darkness.
